# Sample Job Description for Testing

## VP of Digital Transformation - Healthcare

**Company:** Leading Healthcare Network in GCC
**Location:** Dubai, UAE

### About the Role
We are seeking an experienced VP of Digital Transformation to lead our healthcare technology initiatives across the GCC region. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in HealthTech, AI implementation, and hospital operations.

### Key Responsibilities
• Lead digital transformation strategy for 15+ hospitals across GCC
• Oversee AI and automation initiatives to improve patient care and operational efficiency
• Manage PMO function for technology programs with $50M+ budget
• Drive implementation of EMR/EHR systems and clinical decision support tools
• Build and mentor high-performing technology teams
• Collaborate with clinical leaders to align technology with patient outcomes
• Ensure compliance with healthcare regulations and data security standards

### Requirements
• 15+ years of experience in technology leadership roles
• Minimum 5 years in healthcare or HealthTech sector
• Proven experience with AI/ML implementation in healthcare settings
• Strong PMO and program management background
• PMP certification required; MBA preferred
• Experience managing large-scale technology budgets
• Knowledge of healthcare regulations (HIPAA, GDPR, local UAE/KSA requirements)
• Excellent stakeholder management and communication skills

### Preferred Qualifications
• Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP)
• Knowledge of healthcare interoperability standards (HL7, FHIR)
• Background in FinTech or financial services
• Experience working across GCC region
• Arabic language skills (beneficial but not required)

### What We Offer
• Competitive salary: $180,000 - $250,000 USD annually
• Housing allowance
• Medical insurance
• Annual bonus
• Career growth opportunities in expanding healthcare network
